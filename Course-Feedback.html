If you have any suggestions or concerns regarding the quality of this course, please follow this link to let us know about it anonymously.
For final evaluation of the class, please write an essay describing your experience in this class, the strenght and weaknesses, as well as suggestions for improvements. Please consider all relevant areas in your evaluation including the class materials, managements, teaching techniques, and especially methods of on-line teaching. Your essays will be read and analyzed word by word and will be triangulated with other formal and informal anonymous evaluations to ensure validity of the results. Thank you for sharing your experience and thoughts with us!
